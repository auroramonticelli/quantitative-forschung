Course Description

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of quantitative research. We will cover research design, data collection, statistical analysis, and interpretation. By the end, students will be able to design, conduct, and critically assess quantitative studies.

Schedule

  • Week 1: Introduction to Quantitative Research
  • Week 2: Research Design & Hypotheses
  • Week 3: Data Collection Methods
  • Week 4: Descriptive Statistics
  • Week 5: Inferential Statistics
  • Week 6: Regression Analysis
  • Week 7: Validity, Reliability, and Ethics
  • Week 8: Student Presentations
