Purpose

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of quantitative research using R. The focus is on building practical skills for data analysis, visualization, and statistical testing.

Course Overview

In this one-session workshop, students will:

  • Learn how to use R for creating effective data visualizations
  • Understand how to conduct basic statistical tests
  • Interpret results and apply them to real research questions
